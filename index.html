I Could Not Find A "HERO" In My Life, So I Became One.

"Muniba Mazari"

"Mubina Mazari" is known as Iron-Lady and true face of Pakistan. She is a role model and source of inspiration for all of us that limitations are just an illuision in our mind and nothing in this world can stop you from achiving your goals.


  • Born : 3 March 1987 (age 31)
  • Nationality : Pakistani
  • Occupation : Artist, motivational speaker, model
  • Children : 1

EARLY LIFE

Muniba Mazari belongs to a Baloch family. She was born in Rahim Yar Khan. Mazari got her legs paralyzed in an accident when she was heading to her hometown Rahim Yar Khan, her husband fell asleep and hit the car. This accident didn't soften her spirit to help others. She is also a philanthropist and she established the brand which is known as Muniba's Canvas. The slogan of her brand is Let Your Walls Wear Colors.

Career

Muniba Mazari is a first wheelchair anchor. She was an occasional anchor and hosted a show on PTV. Mazari was selected as an inspirational woman in 2015 by BBC. Toni&Guy hired her as a model in Pakistan which makes her first wheelchair-using model. In 2016, She was featured in the Forbes 30 under 30. Mazari is currently a Brand Ambassador for RYTHM Foundation of QI as well as for UN Women Pakistan. She is a motivational speaker and had participated in various forums and conferences. She is also a CSR head for Chughtai Labs.
